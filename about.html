About

The Design Studio 

Our design studio is a fun,creative place where new ideas, discussions, people and a pile of sticky notes merge.

The founders 

The Design studio was the brain child of the brother sister designer duo Kunjel and Vivek. Both trained from National Institute of Design(NID) and having worked on projects for companies like Microsoft, TAT, Hewlett Packard, Sharp Corporation, Intel and Tata among others. Kunjel also did her Masters at Carnegie Mellon University to stir more creative juices. Vivek now works for Jaguar cars as a designer to fulfill his childhood dreams of designing fast and very expensive things.
