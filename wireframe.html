Client

Tuskers web dev

Details

  • Situation Design on workflow that enables users to list their items on a merchant seller portal. The sellers can range from first-time users to established companies. Client noted a significant drop-off rate on this form.Redesign of the section to reduce the drop-off rate.

  • Strategy
    • Identify the pitfalls of their exsiting design: Too many fields in the form can intimidate many people. Unclear instructions. Inconsistency in the data types and UI elements throughout the form.
  • Result
    • Fields been streamlined to appear fewer. Clear instructions and suggestions for fields. User testing of new design showed at least 30% more retainment rate of merchants in a single session.
